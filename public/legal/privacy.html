How XP Reward handles data

We collect name, email address, and profile image from Google OAuth, plus limited IP and device data used for fraud prevention, task verification, and platform security.

What we store

User profile data, XP balances, task history, withdrawals, referral activity, and fraud monitoring signals.

Why we store it

Data is used to operate rewards, validate conversions, prevent duplicate abuse, fulfill withdrawals, and improve platform security.

Third-party links

Task offers may redirect to partner networks. Those partners maintain their own data policies, which users should review before completing an offer.

Contact and controls

Users may request account review or policy clarification from the platform administrator.
